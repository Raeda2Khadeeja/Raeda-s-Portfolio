Movies of All time according to Khadeeja!

My 3 most favourite films

Prestige

A Christopher Nolan classic that keeps you on your toes till the very end.

The Blind Side

A beautiful story based on the true life of a promising African American athlete who is taken in and cared for by a strong willed, loving woman who changes his life.

Pachuvum Athbutha Vilakkum

A perfect pick for some feel good vibes. A story of an ordinary man living a rather simple life who encounters a bunch of extraordinary events.
